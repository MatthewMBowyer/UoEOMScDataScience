Data, analytics and applied artificial intelligence

Building analytical systems that support better decisions

“Qui ne tente rien n'a rien.”

I am an Analytics and Data Science Manager with seven years of experience across business intelligence, operational analytics, financial analysis, data science and reporting automation.

I currently lead global analytics and reporting delivery for senior stakeholders across finance, operations, telematics, research and development, and executive leadership. My work involves translating complex business requirements into scalable analytical solutions, managing analysts and developing automated reporting, forecasting, anomaly detection and machine learning applications.

My technical foundation includes Python, SQL, Tableau, AWS and data pipeline development. I am particularly interested in applied artificial intelligence, multimodal systems, vision-language models, retrieval-augmented generation and AI-enabled decision-support systems.

Alongside my professional work, I am completing an MSc in Data Science through the University of Essex Online. My dissertation explores a multimodal driver behaviour evaluation system that combines telematics, vision-language models and large language models.

Professional direction

My long-term objective is to work at the intersection of data science, artificial intelligence, telematics and sport. I am particularly interested in environments such as motorsport, where high-frequency data, engineering, operational strategy and human performance converge.

I aim to contribute to analytical systems that improve vehicle performance, driver safety, strategic decision-making and operational efficiency. This includes research into multimodal AI systems capable of combining numerical, visual and textual information.

As my career develops, I intend to continue building and leading high-performing analytical teams while mentoring early-career data professionals and delivering practical, responsible AI systems with measurable organisational value.
